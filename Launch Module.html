Launching into Cyber Security

This module was focused on the basic principles of Cyber Security

Learning Outcomes

Numerous topics were analyzed during the span of the module. The topics I have chosen to engage through the 2 collaborative discussions are the following:

    Collaborative Discussion 1:
  • Malicious cyber activities during the Covid-19 era.
  • Awareness activities and trainings as a security measure.
  • Cryptocurrencies used as a money-laundering mechanism.

The feedback from the tutor was vital in order to understand which topics should I try to improve.

    Collaborative Discussion 2:
  • Implementation of a data classification policy & data leak prevention mechanisms.
  • Encryption as a requirement for compliance.
  • Benefits of a Privilege Access Management mechanism implementation.
  • Issues against Next-Generation-Firewalls.

The feedback from the tutor stated my improvements based on my contribution to the discussion, presenting further topics development.

Individual Essay & Assessment Project

The requirement of the essay was to provide critical information regarding the threats an appointment and scheduling management information system is facing.


My essay focused on well-known vulnerabilities of OWASP Top 10 against web applications, and included relative solutions against each vulnerability.
Additionally, for the assessment project, an application was provided through Codio, which I tried to solve vulnerabilties related to OWASP Top 10, by implementing a basic authentication mechanism, different rights between the users and sanitazation as a security mechanism against types of injections.

Reflections

    Through my research, I have managed to learn more regarding:
  • Cryptocurrencies and how cyber criminals can use them for numeral illegal activities.
  • The importance of trainings and awareness activities as a security measure, and ways to implement.
  • Regulatory bodies which require encryption as a security control for the protection of personal data.
  • Marketing traps someone could face while trying to select a Next-Generation-Firewall.

Professional Skills Matrix and Action Plan

In cyber security the topics to learn more about are countless, as we have new additions in such a rapid pace.


However, during the module the most important skill I acquired was time management. Thus, I did not manage to fully achieve every requirement of the deliverables cause of my heavy workload, I understood the lack of the soft skill and tried to improve.
As an action plan, my goal is to continue improving my time management soft skill, and depending the needs of the following modules investigate further which professional skills could I improve and at which order.
